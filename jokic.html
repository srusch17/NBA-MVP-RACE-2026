Nikola Jokić

MVP Ladder Candidate Denver Nuggets

MVP Case

Nikola Jokić remains one of the strongest MVP candidates because of his elite all-around game. He impacts every possession with scoring, rebounding, and playmaking, and he continues to be the engine of Denver’s offense. His efficiency, basketball IQ, and ability to control the pace of the game make him one of the most valuable players in the league.

Key Strengths

  • Playmaking: One of the best passing big men in NBA history
  • Scoring Efficiency: Scores at a high level without forcing shots
  • Rebounding: Controls the glass on both ends
  • Offensive Control: Runs the Nuggets offense through decision-making and vision

Outlook

What a special player. Jokić averaged a triple-double and did so more spectacularly than last season, when he posted the feat. The reason this was a level-up was obvious: Jokić became the first player ever to lead the league in rebounding and assists in the same season. He missed 17 games and still led the league in total assists.
