Victor Wembanyama

MVP Ladder Candidate San Antonio Spurs

MVP Case

Wembanyama announced his arrival as a serious MVP candidate by excelling at both ends, better than anyone in the league. This improved two-way ability and impact was the primary reason the 62-win Spurs were transformed into an instant championship threat. All that was missing from Wembanyama’s season was a historic flavor that was evident with Gilgeous-Alexander and Jokić.

Key Strengths

  • Rim Protection: Elite shot-blocking and paint defense
  • Two-Way Versatility: Impacts both offense and defense at a high level
  • Length and Mobility: Covers huge areas of the court
  • Rising Offensive Game: Improving scoring, shooting, and ball skills

Outlook

If Wembanyama continues developing as a complete superstar and keeps dominating on defense, he has a real chance to stay in the MVP conversation for years to come. His ceiling is one of the highest in the league.
